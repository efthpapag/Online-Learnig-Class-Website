Online learning class at a Glance

Online learning class is a MOOC platform (Massive Open Online Courses) where you can choose your field of study and see which class is relavant to you.
Our programs attract students from all around the world due to their accessibility and convinince.
The high quality of staff, many of whom have been educated abroad and the structure of our programs make for an international institution at the forefront of regional and global education and research.

Content

Online learning class supports content for thematic areas with at least 4 fiels of study for each.
Every field of study includes 5 classes that a student can take.
Online Courses are offered through flexible and blended learning sessions, matching both the specific industry and profession's needs as well as the individual's availability.
